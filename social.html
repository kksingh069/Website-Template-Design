Social Connect

Christmas Donation Drive

Christmas is a time for giving and spreading joy to those in need. In the spirit of the season, SR Realty organized a cloth donation and food distribution drive. All Elitians participated in this noble cause and donated gently used clothes and non-perishable food items. The donation included local areas of Gurugram that are home to needy people, ensuring that those less fortunate can enjoy their holiday season as well.

Our diligent team took the initiative to collect and classify freshly laundered clothes to distribute them among underprivileged children residing in impoverished slum areas. As a socially responsible company, we firmly believe in giving back to the community and this is just one of the numerous ways in which we are determined to make a positive impact. We have wholeheartedly encouraged all members of our team to actively participate in this noble cause and spread love and joy among those who need it the most.
